Challenge a Friend: Invite your friends for a friendly Jiu Jitsu match. It's a perfect opportunity to enjoy the sport you love while contributing to a great cause.

Register Your Roll: After your match, visit our website to register your roll. Simply log in and provide the details of your match.

Donate: The loser of the match, or both participants by mutual agreement, donates a predetermined sum of money. This donation supports various sports charity projects across the globe, helping to provide resources, training, and opportunities for underprivileged communities.

Make an Impact: Your contribution helps to promote the values of sportsmanship, discipline, and community through sports. From building sports facilities to funding training programs for young athletes, your donations make a tangible difference.

Why Participate?

Support a Good Cause: Your donations go directly to supporting sports charity projects that empower and inspire communities worldwide.

Strengthen Bonds: Engage in friendly competition with your peers, fostering camaraderie and mutual respect.

Promote Jiu Jitsu: Help spread the benefits of Jiu Jitsu, including physical fitness, mental discipline, and personal growth.

Register your match, roll for a cause, and help us bring the joy and benefits of sports to those who need it most.
